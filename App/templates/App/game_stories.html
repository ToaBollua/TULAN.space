Game Stories

The Founding of Tulandia

Tulandia was founded by a group of like-minded capsuleers who sought to create a community based on the principles of communism and collective ownership. Our story begins with the founding of the corporation and the early struggles of our members as they worked to build a new home in the vast expanse of New Eden.

The Great Heist

In a daring heist, a group of Tulandia's most skilled pilots infiltrated a heavily guarded Amarr station and made off with a valuable cargo of rare minerals. The heist was a major coup for the corporation and helped to establish Tulandia as a force to be reckoned with in the world of New Eden.

The Battle of Hek

Tulandia's pilots clashed with a rival corporation in a fierce battle over control of the Hek system. Despite being outnumbered, Tulandia's pilots emerged victorious, securing a major victory for the corporation and cementing its reputation as a formidable force in the world of New Eden.
